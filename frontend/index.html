    Help


    • Scan items by entering the barcode or scanning the barcode with the barcode scanner.
    • Add items to the cart by tapping the "+" button to increase the quantity.
    • Remove items from the cart by tapping the "-" button to decrease the quantity.
    • Add a coupon by tapping the "Add Coupon" button and entering the coupon code.
    • Apply the coupon by tapping the "Apply" button.
    • Cancel the coupon by tapping the "Cancel" button.
    • Login as customer by tapping the "Login as Customer" button.
    • Enter your customer credentials to login.
    • Proceed to payment by tapping the "Pay →" button.
    • Cancel the payment by tapping the "x" button in the top right corner.
    • Choose your payment method by tapping the desired payment method.
    • Proceed with the payment by tapping the "Proceed with payment" button.
